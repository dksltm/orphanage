BAITUL SAKINAH AS-SHARIFF

Baitul Sakinah As-Shariff was established in 2011 as Persatuan Kebajikan Anak Yatim, Fakir Miskin & Mualaf Baitul Sakinah As-Shariff (PERKAYFMM). It is a Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) that was registered under the Registrar of Societies (R.O.S). Baitul Sakinah As-Shariff aims to ensure the welfare of the orphans, needy and converts are guaranteed.

These children receive education and care, as well as physical, mental, and spiritual discipline. Hajjah Fatimah Abdullah Care Centres are owned and run by Sakinah PERKAYFMM Baitul - Shariff in Kampung Baru, Port Klang. In 2014, the social welfare department registered the orphanage. 30 persons can be accommodated at once at Klang Care Centre.In addition to having exceptional academic performance, Sakinah PERKAYFMM Baitul-Shariff hopes that these kids will behave honourably towards their country, religion, and government.

As of now, in 2023, Baitul Sakinah As-Shariff is a home for around 20 children.
